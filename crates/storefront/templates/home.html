{# Main story text #}

Naked Pineapple was born from the vision and passion of Kristi Fry, a licensed aesthetician with over 20 years of experience in the skincare industry.

With her deep knowledge and hands-on expertise, Kristi played a vital role in the formulation of each Naked Pineapple product, ensuring they deliver real results while honoring all skin types.

Kristi's mission goes beyond skincare—she's driven to empower and uplift everyone she meets. Her genuine warmth and dedication to building confidence shine through in everything Naked Pineapple stands for. Kristi believes that true beauty radiates from within and that skincare is a powerful tool to nurture self-love.

Through Naked Pineapple, Kristi invites you to embark on a journey of glowing skin, authentic confidence, and unwavering empowerment. Because everyone deserves to feel radiant in their own skin.
