Questions?

Subscription FAQ

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! There are no commitments or contracts. You can cancel, pause, or skip deliveries at any time through your account dashboard.
How do I change my delivery schedule?
Log into your account and visit the Subscriptions page. From there, you can adjust your delivery frequency, skip upcoming orders, or change your next delivery date.
Can I add or remove products from my subscription?
Absolutely! You can modify your subscription products at any time before your next billing date. Add new favorites or remove products you no longer need.
When will I be charged?
You'll be charged when you place your initial order and then on your selected schedule (every 30, 60, or 90 days). We'll always send you a reminder email before each charge.
